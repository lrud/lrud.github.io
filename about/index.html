I’m Lukas Rueda, a quantitative analyst completing my M.A. in Economics at Hunter College. My research focuses on financial econometrics and machine learning applications for cryptocurrency derivatives markets, particularly Bitcoin volatility forecasting using LSTM neural networks.

Current Focus

My master’s thesis develops a comprehensive 17-model benchmark framework for Bitcoin implied volatility (DVOL) prediction. The research combines traditional econometric methods (HAR-RV, GARCH) with modern deep learning (LSTM), achieving 92.87% R² on test data with a key discovery of DataParallel evaluation artifacts in multi-GPU training.
